class MODEL\+::\+Blood\+Vessel$<$ T $>$}
Resistor-\/capacitor-\/inductor blood vessel with optional stenosis. 

Models the mechanical behavior of a bloodvessel with optional stenosis.

\[

node[anchor=south]{$P_{out}$}
(5,0) to [C, l=$C$, -] (5,-1.5)
node[ground]{};
\draw [-latex] (7.2,0) -- (8,0) node[right] {$Q_{out}$};
\end{circuitikz}

\[
P_{in}^{e}-P_{out}^{e}-(R+R_{ste}) Q_{in}^{e}-L\frac{d Q_{out}^{e}}{dt}=0
\]

\[
Q_{i n}^{e}-Q_{o u t}^{e}-C \frac{d P_{in}^{e}}{d t}+C(R+2R_{ste})\frac{d
Q_{in}^{e}}{d t}=0 \]\hypertarget{class_m_o_d_e_l_1_1_blood_vessel_autotoc_md1}{}\doxyparagraph{Local contributions}\label{class_m_o_d_e_l_1_1_blood_vessel_autotoc_md1}
\[
\mathbf{y}^{e}=\left[\begin{array}{llll}P_{i n}^{e} & Q_{in}^{e} &
P_{out}^{e} & Q_{out}^{e}\end{array}\right]^{T} \]

\[
\mathbf{F}^{e}=\left[\begin{array}{cccc}
1 & -R_{ste}-R & -1 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 & -1
\end{array}\right]
\]

\[
\mathbf{E}^{e}=\left[\begin{array}{cccc}
0 & 0 & 0 & -L \\
-C & C(R+2R_{ste}) & 0 & 0
\end{array}\right]
\]

\[
\mathbf{D}^{e}=\left[\begin{array}{cccc}
0 & -R_{ste} & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 2CK_{ste} sgn(Q_{in}^{e}) \dot{Q}_{in}^{e} & 0 & 0
\end{array}\right]
\]

with the stenosis resistance $ R_{ste}=K_{t} \frac{\rho}{2
A_{o}^{2}}\left(\frac{A_{o}}{A_{s}}-1\right)^{2}|Q_{in}^{e}| $. The constant part of the equation is summarized in Parameters\+::stenosis\+\_\+coefficient. $R$, $C$, and $L$ refer to Poisieuille resistance, capacitance and inductance, respectively.\hypertarget{class_m_o_d_e_l_1_1_blood_vessel_autotoc_md2}{}\doxyparagraph{Gradient}\label{class_m_o_d_e_l_1_1_blood_vessel_autotoc_md2}

C\dot{y}_2 & (-\dot{y}_1+(R+2R_{ste})\dot{y}_2) & 0 & 2C|y_2|\dot{y}_2
\end{array}\right]
